About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly Bombay, is a historic coastal city in India. Its past includes periods of Portuguese and British colonial rule. Today, Mumbai stands as India's financial and entertainment hub, boasting a skyline filled with skyscrapers, a thriving Bollywood film industry, and a bustling stock exchange. Despite modernization, the city retains its rich cultural diversity, evident in its cuisine, architecture, and population. Mumbai's story is a compelling blend of heritage and contemporary progress.
