About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of the Indian state of Telangana, is a captivating metropolis known for its rich heritage, vibrant culture, and modern developments. Steeped in history, the city boasts architectural marvels such as the Golconda Fort, with its remarkable acoustics, and the iconic Charminar, an imposing monument that has become a symbol of Hyderabad. However, Hyderabad is not just a relic of the past; it's a dynamic city with a booming IT industry, earning it the nickname "Cyberabad." HITEC City, a technological hub within the city, houses numerous multinational corporations and startups, making Hyderabad a significant player in India's tech landscape.
