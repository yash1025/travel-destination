About Kolkata

Kolkata, the capital of West Bengal, is a city that encapsulates India's rich history, vibrant culture, and intellectual heritage. Situated gracefully along the banks of the Hooghly River, Kolkata is a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be explored. Once known as Calcutta, it boasts a unique blend of colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and a thriving arts scene. The city played a pivotal role in India's fight for independence and remains a symbol of resilience and determination. Kolkata is renowned for its literary luminaries, with luminaries like Rabindranath Tagore and Satyajit Ray calling it home. The iconic Victoria Memorial stands as a testament to the city's colonial past, while the annual Durga Puja festival transforms the streets into a colorful carnival of art and culture. From the bustling markets of New Market to the intellectual haven of College Street, the city offers diverse experiences for shoppers and scholars alike.
